# IDEAS-Lab

## Indicators, Data, Epidemiology and Social Analysis

IDEAS-Lab is a research group at the [Department of Statistical Sciences](https://www.stat.unipd.it/en/) of the [University of Padova](https://www.unipd.it/en). Our work focuses on the development and application of statistical methods for the analysis of social and epidemiological phenomena, with particular attention to indicators and data integration in public health.

Website (under development): https://ideas.stat.unipd.it

IDEAS-Lab brings together researchers in statistics with expertise in social statistics, epidemiology and data science. The group aims to contribute to the understanding of complex societal challenges through rigorous quantitative methods and interdisciplinary collaboration. The lab is affiliated with the Department of Statistical Sciences at the University of Padova and collaborates with national and international institutions.
